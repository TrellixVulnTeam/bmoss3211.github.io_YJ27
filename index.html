About Me

When I was seven years old I got my hands on my first video game system. When I was fourteen I decided I wanted to develop these experiences as a living. Today, as I finish my undergraduate education, I am excited to see what possibilities the ever-growing field of game development holds, and what my part will be in all of it. In my free time I enjoy playing instruments, singing, and reading comic books. Developing games is my passion, and the joyous feeling I get when making them is very similar to the feeling I had when I was seven holding that controller for the very first time.
